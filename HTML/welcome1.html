What is automation?

Automation refers to the use of systems or technological elements that perform processes and tasks without human intervention, with the aim of increasing efficiency, reducing errors, and saving time and resources.

MYTHS

Automation will eliminate all jobs

One of the most widespread myths is that automation will result in massive job losses. While it is true that some jobs may be replaced, automation also creates new jobs and can improve the quality of existing jobs by eliminating repetitive tasks and allowing workers to focus on more strategic and creative aspects of their work.

Automation is only for large companies

Another myth is that only large corporations can benefit from automation due to its cost and complexity. Nowadays, there are automation solutions available for businesses of all sizes, including small and medium-sized enterprises, which can be scalable and adjustable to different needs and budgets.

Automation only affects manufacturing

Although automation has a significant impact on the manufacturing industry, its use extends to many other sectors, including services, healthcare, agriculture, finance, and more. Almost any process that requires consistency and repetition is a candidate for automation.

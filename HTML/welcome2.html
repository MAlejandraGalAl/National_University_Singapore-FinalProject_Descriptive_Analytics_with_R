Understanding the industry helps you identify key skills and knowledge needed to advance in your career. It also allows you to keep up with trends and changes that may impact your current and future role. Industries evolve over time, influenced by technology, policies, and social changes. Understanding the industry helps you anticipate these changes and adapt your skills and business strategies to maintain competitiveness.
